Privacy Policy — Transfer Wifi

Last updated: May 16, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Transfer Wifi is designed to work without an internet connection and without external servers. The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

The only data processed is:

  • Files selected by the user for transfer (local processing only, never sent to our servers)
  • App preferences (theme, save folder, PIN) — stored locally on the device via SharedPreferences
  • Premium purchase status — managed exclusively by Google Play Billing

2. Network and Communications

Transfers are performed directly between devices via:

  • Local Wi-Fi network: peer-to-peer connection without going through the internet
  • Wi-Fi Direct: standard Android protocol, no third-party servers
  • PC Share: local HTTP server on your phone, accessible only on your local network

No data transits through our servers.

3. Advertisements (Free version)

The free version displays advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect data according to its own Google Privacy Policy. Premium users see no advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are managed by Google Play Billing. Transfer Wifi does not receive your payment information. Google processes this data in accordance with its payment terms.

5. Required Permissions

  • Wi-Fi / Network: to discover devices and transfer files
  • Storage: to read and write transferred files
  • Approximate Location: required by Android to use Wi-Fi Direct (no location data is recorded)
  • Notifications: to display transfer progress
  • Biometrics: optional, to lock access to the application

6. Children's Data

Transfer Wifi does not collect any data and does not target children under the age of 13.

7. Changes

Any changes to this policy will be published on this page. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact
